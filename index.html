📖 The Story
In the peaceful town of Pallet Town, a young trainer named Alex is finally old enough to start a Pokémon journey. But Professor Oak has one condition first...

"The Pokémon world is full of choices," Oak explains. "How many ways can a trainer build a team? To receive your starter Pokémon, you must prove you understand the mathematics of counting!"

Alex sets off across 8 iconic Kanto locations — each hiding a counting puzzle. Solve them all, earn 8 Counting Badges, and Professor Oak will crown you a Pokémon Math Master! 🏆
