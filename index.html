📖 THE STORY

In the peaceful town of Pallet Town, young trainer Alex has just turned 10 — old enough to begin a Pokémon journey. But Professor Oak has a special task before handing over a starter Pokémon...

"The Pokémon world is full of choices," Oak explains, adjusting his glasses. "How many ways can a trainer build a team? How many battle strategies exist? The answers lie in the mathematics of counting."

Alex must journey across 8 Challenge Zones — from Viridian Forest to Indigo Plateau — solving counting problems that arise naturally from Pokémon adventures. Each solved problem earns a Counting Badge, proving Alex is ready to become a Pokémon Master AND a Combinatorics Champion!
